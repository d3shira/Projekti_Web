Here are some of the hotels we recommend you staying while visiting Kosovo



Hotel Dukagjini

Centrally located in Pejë, Hotel Dukagjini features modern accommodation, an indoor pool, steam bath and a gym. Guests can enjoy dining in 3 on-site restaurants. Free Wi-Fi and a private parking are provided. Elegantly decorated rooms and suites are fitted with an LCD cable TV, a balcony and comfortable seating area. Some suites come with an on-site sauna and Swarovski chandeliers. The spacious bathroom comprises toiletries, bathrobe and slippers.

Hotel Kulla e Zenel Beut

Located in Pejë, Hotel - Kulla e Zenel Beut provides accommodation with a terrace, free private parking, a restaurant and a bar. The hotel features a sauna, room service and free WiFi. The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Each room is fitted with a private bathroom with a bidet, a hairdryer and slippers. Guest rooms at Hotel - Kulla e Zenel Beut include air conditioning and a desk.

Hotel Panorama

Located in Peje, Hotel Panorama Rugove provides accommodation with a garden, free private parking, a terrace and a restaurant. Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground. The accommodation features room service and free WiFi. At the hotel, every room comes with a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen and towels. All units will provide guests with a fridge.

Sky Hotel

Located in Prizren, 1.5 km from Sinan Pasha Mosque, Sky Hotel provides accommodation with a terrace, free private parking, a restaurant and a bar. The air-conditioned rooms provide a city view and come with a desk and free WiFi. At the hotel, every room is fitted with a balcony with a mountain view. All units in Sky Hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a hairdryer.

Hotel Prior

Situated in Prizren, 600 m from Sinan Pasha Mosque, Prior Hotel features accommodation with a garden, free private parking, a terrace and a restaurant. With a bar, the 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. The accommodation provides room service, a 24-hour front desk and currency exchange for guests.

Hotel Tesara

Set in Prizren, 3.2 km from Sinan Pasha Mosque, Hotel Tesara offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking and a bar. This 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and free WiFi. Albanian League of Prizren Museum is 3.2 km away and Kalaja Fortress Prizren is 3.4 km from the hotel. All guest rooms in the hotel are fitted with a kettle. At Hotel Tesara, the rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a safety deposit box.

Premium Apartments

Premium Apartments has city views, free WiFi and free private parking, set in Gjakovë.Each unit features an equipped kitchen, a living room with a sofa bed and flat-screen TV and private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. All units are air conditioned and include a seating and/or dining area.

Garden Guesthouse

Boasting garden views, Garden Guesthouse provides accommodation with a terrace and a patio, around 34 km from Mirusha Waterfalls. The property features a garden and free private parking. The air-conditioned holiday home consists of 1 bedroom, a living room, a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge and a kettle, and 1 bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

Hotel Happy

Hotel Happy features a shared lounge, terrace, a restaurant and bar in Gjakove. This 3-star hotel offers room service, luggage storage spaceand free WiFi. Certain rooms at the property include a balcony with a city view.The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms with a desk, a kettle, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bidet. At Hotel Happy each room includes bed linen and towels.
