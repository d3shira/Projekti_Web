Is Kosovo safe for tourists?

Kosovo is a very small country.Safety is one of the main concerns while travelling. So no wander people are asking themselves is Kosovo safe to visit. The short answer to this question would be yes, it is. In fact, it is one of the safest in the world! The people of Kosovo are known to be very warm and welcoming to tourists!

What is Kosovo most known for?

Kosovo is the youngest country in Europe and the second youngest country in the whole world. It is a small rectangular shaped country located in the center of the Balkan’s as it connects a lot of countries with one another. Kosovo is known for it’s beautiful views and welcoming people.

Why should I visit Kosovo?

Kosovo is a land of remote, rugged mountains, home to ferociously independent people and stunningly untouched beauty.
